Who We Are

Institute of Diesel & Heavy Equipment Fittings TVET

A high-quality technical-vocational institution focused on practical training and character competence. Empowering the next generation of technicians through rigorous technical training and industry-aligned curricula. We bridge the gap between education and high-value employment.
